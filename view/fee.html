    Trading fees

  • General: 0.1% trading fee.
  • By default, if you hold BXB in your account, your trading fees will be automatically subtracted from your BXB balance. Using BXB for transaction fees applies a 0% discount to your trading fee will be applied (subject to change). Read more
  • Trading fees are determined based on trading volume over a 30-day period (in BTC) and BXB balance.
  • At 00:00 AM (UTC) every day, your trading volume over the past 30-day period and your current BXB balance are evaluated. Your Tier level and corresponding maker/taker fees are updated one hour later.
