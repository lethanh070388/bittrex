ABOUT BITRADEX

Founded in 2017 by four cybersecurity engineers, BITRADEX is the cryptocurrencies based blockchain platform, providing lightning-fast trade execution, dependable digital wallets and industry-leading security practices. Our mission is to help advance the blockchain industry by fostering innovation, incubating new and emerging technology, and driving transformative change.

WHAT MAKES BITRADEX DIFFERENT?

Secure, Safety and Stability platform – BITRADEX employs the most reliable, effective security technologies available. We leverage an elastic, multi-stage wallet strategy to ensure that the majority of funds are kept in cold storage for additional safety. Also, BITRADEX enables two-factor authentication for all users and provides a host of additional security features to provide multiple layers of protection. At BITRADEX, security will always be a top priority in every decision we make.

High Performance – Capable of processing more than 1,500,000 orders per second

Fast deposits and withdrawals – Our highly efficient and automated monitoring platform allows us to provide users the fastest transactions available today. This includes updates on balance, trade, and wallet information.

High Liquidity – we have many partners and agents and thousands of private accounts

Driving Innovation – To help drive innovation in the blockchain industry, BITRADEX is committed to supporting both new and established blockchains. BITRADEX seeks to provide its users with an ever-growing selection of blockchain technologies and digital tokens, and all new digital tokens listed on the trading platform must complete a rigorous review process.

All Devices Covered – we have supported Web, Mobile Web, Windows, macOS

Commitment to compliance – BITRADEX is committed to complying to detect and remediate unlawful behavior by customers and virtual currency developers when using the BITRADEX trading platform or any of the company’s other services.

Multilingual Support – Support and FAQs avaible in multiple languages

Multiple-Coin Support – BTC, ETH, LTC, DASH,…
